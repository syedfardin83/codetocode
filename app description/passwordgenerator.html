How to Download

Click on the download button given above, which will redirect you to a media fire page.Click on the download button. Extract the .rar file which was downloaded. Then open the .exe file. Click on 'Yes' if any permission is asked. Click on next on the Installer window. Click on install. Done! Now open the app by either clicking on the start menu or on clicking on the icon on the desktop.

Usage of the app

To use this app is very simple. First you have to open the app by either clicking on the icon on the desktop or from the start menu. After you have opened the app, you will see a 'Generate' button. Click on that button; Now, you will se the generated password in a grey coloured rectangle as show above in the screenshots.

Overview of the app

This is a very simple password generator which generates a password of 10 characters. This is a very strong password generator as it is nearly impossible for the hackers to know your password using a wordlist.
