This Quiz app is made out of pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Overview of the app

There will be 6 questions asked and 4 options will be provided for each question. For every correct answer, you will be awarded 10 points, and for every wrong answer, there will be no marks awarded. You can see your score the whole time in the top right corner of the page.

After you have answered all the questions, You will get an end screen where you can save your score by writing your name and clicking on save. Now please note that the highscores are stored on local storage and not on the web. You cannot see your saved highscore on any other device. Based on the device you are using, your saved high scores will be deleted after a certain time.

Now there are only about 20 questions in total and any 6 random questions will be show each time you play the game.
